# Design System

## Tone

Modern, simple, direct, trustworthy, sober, accessible, and mobile-first.

Avoid generic legal stock imagery, gavels, courtrooms, handshakes, excessive gradients, carousels, invasive pop-ups, videos, 3D effects, and distracting animations.

## Colors

- Navy: `#01273D`
- Gold: `#C9A459`
- White: `#FFFFFF`
- Light gray: `#D2DCDF`
- Dark graphite: used for body text

Gold is used sparingly for thin borders, small accents, and CTA emphasis.

## Brand Mark

The Phase 1 mark is temporary and replaceable. It uses the text `Almeida Junior Advogado` and a simple original scales-of-justice symbol. It does not use the official OAB symbol.

## Layout

- Mobile-first sections.
- Header with accessible anchor navigation.
- Hero with direct value proposition and visible CTA.
- Visual contact form above the fold on desktop.
- Comfortable touch targets and visible focus states.
- No nested decorative cards.

## Typography

Use restrained, legible typography. Headings should be clear and proportional to their containers. Body text should prioritize readability.

## Accessibility

Validation must combine automated checks, browser inspection, Lighthouse, and manual review. Manual review should cover keyboard navigation, focus visibility, labels, touch targets, heading order, and contrast.
